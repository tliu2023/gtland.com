GTLand is a paper quilling art shop owned by Carol, who lives in Texas.

She became interested in this traditional form of paper art in 2019 and practiced it as a hobby. Since then, she has made hundreds of pieces and thoroughly enjoys doing so. Every art piece is 100% handmade from 5mm paper strips and made with precision. Carol first recolors each strip with soft pastels for vibrant colors true to the piece's model and arranges them onto a board background. She glues them on, ensuring the piece presents a smooth color transition throughout. Carol aims to present flora, fish and birds with a different style than what people are used to. Her art pieces have been privately collected around the USA.
